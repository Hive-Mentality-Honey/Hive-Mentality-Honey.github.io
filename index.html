Small-batch beekeeping from Charlotte, NC

About Us

Hive Mentality Honey is a small beekeeping operation based in Charlotte, North Carolina. We are passionate about sustainable beekeeping, healthy hive management, and sharing the craft with fellow beekeepers.

Founded by experienced beekeepers with over a decade in the field, we raise honeybees with care and respect for the colony — following best practices for hive health, mite management, and seasonal stewardship.

Our App

We build tools to help beekeepers manage their hives more effectively.

BeeSpace — Hive Management

A hive inspection and management app for backyard and hobbyist beekeepers. Track inspections, monitor mite counts, log feedings, and manage your apiary from your phone.
